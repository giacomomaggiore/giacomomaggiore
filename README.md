# Giacomo Maggiore

I was born and raised in [**Milan**](https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milano), and since I was a child, I’ve had a strong passion for numbers, technology and physics.  

After high school, I studied [*Automation Engineering*](https://www.polimi.it/formazione/corsi-di-laurea/dettaglio-corso/ingegneria-dellautomazione) at [**Politecnico di Milano**](https://www.polimi.it/), spending one semester abroad in Sweden, as an exchange student at [**Mälardalen University**](https://www.mdu.se/en/malardalen-university).  

Shortly after, I co-founded [*SceneSnap*](https://scenesnap.co/), a B2B and B2C **EdTech startup** that develops learning tools by bridging artificial intelligence and data analytics.  

During my studies, I deep dived into **financial markets** and **personal finance**, reason why I moved to [*ETH Zurich*](https://mtec.ethz.ch/studies.html), where I’m currently pursuing a Master in [**Economics, Management & Technology**](https://mtec.ethz.ch/studies.html), focusing on the most quantitative and analytically oriented courses.  

I’m deeply interested in **financial risk management**, **quantitative finance**, and **personal finance**.  
Alongside my studies, I work as a research assistant at [*KOF*](https://kof.ethz.ch/it/), the Swiss Economic Institute, where I further strengthen my analytical and research skills.  

When I'm not working or studying, you can find me **running** and **cycling**.  
In 2024 I ran [*my first marathon*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vIGYlnaqcP8&t=451s) and completed my first ultramarathon, sharing my journey on [*YouTube*](https://www.youtube.com/@giacomomaggiore01) and [*Strava*](https://www.strava.com/athletes/24804406).  

Beyond running, I enjoy outdoor activities and love to travel in a rough and itinerant way, always looking for unusual and cheap destinations, far from tourist hotspots.
